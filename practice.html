Welcome to your journey of self-discovery. Inspired by the Samudra Manthan, this 6-module practice guides you to "churn your inner ocean," reflecting on your strengths (Devas) and challenges (Asuras), confronting toxic emotions, and embracing growth. Each module includes a task and reflection to help you gain wisdom and resilience. Complete the journey daily or at your own pace, and track your progress with a streak counter.
