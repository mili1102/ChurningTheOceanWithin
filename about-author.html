About Millie Jess

Millie Jess is a storyteller, spiritual thinker, and seeker of wisdom whose journey began with a deep fascination for the myths that shape human understanding. Inspired by the ancient Hindu epic Samudra Manthan, she weaves its lessons of duality—Devas and Asuras—into a modern guide for personal transformation. Her love for introspection, nurtured through years of reflecting on life’s challenges, led her to author "Churning the Ocean Within."

Growing up, Millie found solace in the ocean’s rhythm, mirroring the mind’s vastness as described in her book. This connection drove her to explore how ancient wisdom can heal modern minds, culminating in practical reflection exercises that empower readers to unlock their inner nectar of success.
