Identify Your Inner Ocean

Take a moment to reflect on your mind as an ocean. Use the questions below to map your thoughts and emotions:

  • Your Ocean (Mind): What thoughts or feelings dominate your daily experience? (e.g., calm, stormy)
  • Devas (Positive Qualities): What strengths or virtues do you notice? (e.g., kindness, clarity)
  • Asuras (Negative Emotions): What challenges or emotions arise? (e.g., fear, doubt)
