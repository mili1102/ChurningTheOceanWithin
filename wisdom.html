Wisdom from Samudra Manthan

In the ancient Hindu epic, the gods and demons churned the Ocean of Milk to obtain Amrita, the nectar of immortality. This process revealed both poison and treasures. Similarly, your mind is an ocean—churning it through reflection uncovers negativity and the clarity, wisdom, and success within.
